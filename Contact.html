Frequently Asked Questions

How long does phone repair usually take?

Most common repairs like screen replacement or battery replacement are completed within 1-2 hours. More complex repairs may take 24-48 hours. We'll provide an estimated completion time when you bring in your device.

Do you offer warranty on repairs?

Yes, all our repairs come with a 90-day warranty covering both parts and labor. This warranty covers any issues related to the repair work we've performed.

Can I trade in my old phone?

Absolutely! We offer competitive trade-in values for most smartphones. Bring your device to our store for a free evaluation and we'll provide an instant quote.

Do you sell unlocked phones?

Yes, we offer a wide selection of unlocked phones that work with any carrier. Our staff can help you choose the right device for your needs and budget.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and bank transfers. We also offer financing options for higher-value purchases.
