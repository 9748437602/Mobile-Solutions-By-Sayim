Our Story Meet Our Team

Our Humble Beginnings

Ms Mobile Solutions started in 2019 as a small kiosk in Lauhati Bazar, Kolkata, with a vision to provide quality mobile devices at affordable prices. Founded by Arif Goldar, our journey began with just two employees and a passion for technology.

What started as a small shop has now grown into one of Kolkata's most trusted mobile solutions providers. Over the years, we've expanded our offerings to include smartphones, tablets, accessories, and repair services, all while maintaining our commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our success is built on the trust of thousands of satisfied customers who have chosen us for their mobile needs. We've stayed true to our core values of quality, affordability, and exceptional service, helping us grow while maintaining our personal touch.

Our Journey

May 2019

Foundation

Ms Mobile Solutions opens its first small kiosk in Lauhati Bazar, Kolkata, with just two employees and a dream to provide quality mobile solutions.

December 2020

First Expansion

We expand to our first full-sized store location in Lauhati Bazar, offering a wider range of products and services to our growing customer base.

July 2021

Service Center Launch

We establish our in-house service center, providing professional repair and maintenance services for all major mobile brands.

March 2022

Online Store Launch

We launch our e-commerce platform, allowing customers across Kolkata to shop our products online with home delivery.

January 2023

Second Location

We open our second store location in Howrah, expanding our reach to serve more customers in the greater Kolkata area.

Today

Serving Kolkata

With two stores, an online platform, and a dedicated team of 15 professionals, we continue to serve Kolkata with passion and dedication.
